Happy Tails Pet Services

At Happy Tails Pet Services, we offer exceptional pet care solutions for your furry, feathered, and scaly friends in Ebbw Vale, South Wales. We are a dedicated, local company passionate about providing your pets with the highest level of care and attention. Whether you need dog walking, pet sitting, or other bespoke pet services, we’re here to ensure your pets are happy, healthy, and well-looked after when you're busy or away.

Dog Walking

At Happy Tails Pet Services, we understand that your furry friends need regular exercise and companionship to stay happy and healthy. That’s why we offer reliable and personalized dog walking services tailored to your pup’s unique needs.

Pet Sitting

We can offer in-home pet boarding to allow your pets to stay in the comfort of their own home environment. We are also able to offer this service for bird and exotic pet care.

Services
Pet Boarding

We can offer dog boarding in our safe and secure heated kennels. Your dogs will have use of our paddock for regular playtime, group walks, or exercise sessions to keep your pets active and engaged.

Pet Grooming

We offer a range of professional services to pets to maintain their cleanliness, health, and overall well-being. These services go beyond simple washing and brushing and can include a variety of treatments designed to improve your pet's appearance and comfort.
